Josephine and Henry Bellizzi

This is Josephine and her family. From left : the nanny  holding baby Harry,  Henry George Bellizzi husband of Josephine who is sitting and is expecting Peter ! Standing in front is May (now Soeur Marie de l’Immaculee). After Peter, 4 other babies were born and died, then Alfred and Elda were born.
