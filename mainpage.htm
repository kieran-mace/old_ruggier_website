My dot com Dream - by Paul Ruggier

This family website has been my dream for years. I famously first announced it in my Best Man’s speech at Michael and Orietta’s wedding in June 2000! For 3 1/2 years it’s been a case of “ www.ruggier.com under development”. Then I met Adrian and Stephanie Patrick, of Webtoil. Late-night emailing, technical wizardry, fresh and delightful design, ultimata for delivery of photos and the hard work has finally paid off! But now that we’re live, we’ve realised this is just the beginning, not an end result. With your input and feedback, we can fill in the gaps in the family history and keep the site lively and topical. The next phase will be to complete the Ansara side of our family which will be on www.ansarafamily.com – but you guessed – it’s “under development”!

Now it’s over to you. Paul
