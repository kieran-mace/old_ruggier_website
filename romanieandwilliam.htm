Romanie Ruggier

Here we all are, looking very happy in 2001. However, Will had just been diagnosed with cancer, and he very sadly died at age 41y, in 2002, when Loic and Luke were only aged 4 and 5y respectively.

2007, Loic and Luke are now aged 10 and 11y, and life goes on… We have great fun and I keep occupied with my brilliant children, my fantastic job as an anaesthetist, my sport which always revolves around the ski season, and my music. I now play the viola regularly in the European Doctor’s Orchestra, which takes us around Europe each year, performing charity concerts there and in London. Loic, Luke and I love travelling and have recently been to Kerala in Southwest India. We are looking forward to another family reunion this Christmas in South Africa.

My current aim is to spend more time doing nothing!
