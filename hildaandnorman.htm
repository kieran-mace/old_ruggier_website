Hilda and Norman McNally

Norman celebrated his 75th birthday on 22 October 2007. We thank God for this gift of life, having recovered from a heart attack in 1994, a subsequent triple by-pass and from a diagnosis of lung cancer earlier this year. Thanks be to God - the cancer is in remission and we pray for this situation to continue. Hilda, who turned 73 on 18th July this year is fit and very well. We thank God again for His blessings. The big news is the forthcoming celebration of our golden wedding anniversary on 16th December this year (2007). How the 50 years have flown. We've been through good times and bad but our love for each other is stronger than ever before. We pray that all of you experience the peace, love and companionship we enjoy in our life today. We have been blessed with wonderful children and grandchildren - can anyone ask for more?

Cheers, Norman & Hilda.
