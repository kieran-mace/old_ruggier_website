About Me

I was born in October 2000, so I’m a millennium baby! When I was two I did a lot of travelling. We went camping in Texas and the lake had alligators in it. We think we saw one! Then we went to South Africa for Christmas to meet lots of relatives and a few weeks later I moved to France. I went to French ‘maternelle’ school, and went to dancing classes with my best friend, Hannah. We used to go to Parc Asterix quite often. When I was nearly five we came to England and I love my school here too but Hannah is still my best friend.
 

My favourite things

I am the princess of ‘lambies’ and everything blue [ it used to be pink]. I love ballet, gymnastics and dancing to the music on Daddy’s iPod. I love to drive the jetski and let the waves go over me and Daddy. I have lots of friends
